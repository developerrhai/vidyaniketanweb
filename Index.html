About Us

About Vidyaaniketan Classes Established in 2017, Vidyaaniketan Classes is a trusted coaching institute in Indapur, Pune, dedicated to guiding students towards academic excellence. We specialize in SSC (Class 7th–10th), HSC (11th–12th), and MHT-CET preparation, helping students build strong concepts and perform confidently in exams. With a focus on clarity, consistency, and results, our teaching approach ensures personal attention, regular assessments, and structured learning for every student. Makes You Confident and Capable Through concept-based learning and regular practice, we help students become confident in their knowledge and capable of achieving their academic goals.
